1. [x] **performance-reviewer** | `src/webserver.cpp:401` | memory-allocation
   When route(method_set{get,post,...}, ...) registers N methods, `handler` is copied N-1 times in the commit loop. `set_slot(m, handler)` passes by value, triggering a std::function copy for each slot beyond the first. For the common N=1 case this is a single copy of the already-passed-by-value parameter; for N=7 it is six extra heap allocations in the std::function small-buffer-overflow path.
   *Recommendation:* Move the handler into the last slot and copy into the others, or pre-copy into a local and move into each slot: change the commit loop to `shim->set_slot(m, (last_bit ? std::move(handler) : handler));`. Alternatively change set_slot to `void set_slot(http_method, lambda_handler&&)` and pass `std::move(handler)` unconditionally for single-method callers, keeping a copy only when N > 1.
   *Status:* Fixed in `src/detail/webserver_routes.cpp`: `commit_handlers_to_shim` now takes handler by value, collects requested methods into a small inline array, copies into N-1 slots and moves into the last slot. Declaration in `webserver_impl_dispatch.hpp` updated to match.
