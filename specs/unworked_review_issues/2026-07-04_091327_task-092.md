6. [x] **performance-reviewer** | `.github/workflows/verify-build.yml:1181` | missing-caching
   RTC_ITERATIONS=8 with timeout-minutes: 2 on the TSan lane is a tight budget. Each route_table_concurrency binary run contains two sub-tests, each with a 30-second wall-clock safety ceiling (lines 130 and 218 of route_table_concurrency.cpp). Under heavy TSan overhead (10-20x) on a loaded ubuntu-latest runner, sub-test 1 alone can take 10-30 seconds (10k writer_ops serialised on route_table_mutex_ at ~0.5-1ms per op under TSan).
